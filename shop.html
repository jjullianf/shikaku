Why a Shikaku puzzle book?

There is something different about solving a puzzle on paper. No screen, no notifications, no battery to worry about. Just a pencil and a grid. Shikaku is one of those puzzles that works particularly well in print — the logic is visual, the rules are simple, and a solved page feels genuinely satisfying to look at.

The book is designed to be picked up and put down. Each puzzle fits on its own page with plenty of space to work in. Whether you are commuting, travelling, or just looking for something to do with your hands, it is the kind of book that earns a spot in a bag.
